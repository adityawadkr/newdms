**Date:** December 8, 2024 | **Status:** Near Completion

## What is AutoFlow?

AutoFlow is a **Dealership Management System (DMS)** — a web-based platform that manages the complete operations of an automobile dealership, from customer walk-in to vehicle delivery and beyond.

## Key Features

### For Sales Team
- Track customers from enquiry to delivery
- Send professional quotations via email
- Schedule and manage test drives
- Book vehicles and track payments

### For Service Department
- Create and assign job cards
- Track workshop jobs from start to completion
- Manage spare parts inventory

### For HR & Management
- Employee database with role management
- Attendance tracking (clock in/out)
- Leave applications and approvals
- Payroll processing

### For Finance
- Invoice generation
- Payment recording
- Revenue and expense visualization
